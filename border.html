The Border Collie is a highly intelligent and energetic breed known for its exceptional herding skills and boundless enthusiasm. Originally bred to work with sheep in the rugged borderlands of England and Scotland, these dogs excel in agility, obedience, and problem-solving. With their striking appearance—often marked by black-and-white coats and piercing eyes—Border Collies are as beautiful as they are hardworking.
